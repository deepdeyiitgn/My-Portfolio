# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove or reject contributions, comments, or interactions that violate these standards.
